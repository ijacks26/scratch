The Total Solar Eclipse

Last week I got a chance to see the total solar eclipse. It was an amazing exerience beyond words, but here are some cool things I learned about solar eclipses.
  1. What makes it special
    • The Sun is 400x larger than the Moon, but 400x farther away
    • The Moon's orbit is eliptical and tilted, so it is much rarer for a total solar eclipse to occur
    • You can look at the sun during totality with you naked eye
  2. What happens during totality
    • You can see solar flares from the Sun, and the corona
    • There is a 360 degree sunset
    • It get much colder
    • Birds stop singing and some start acting strange
